1. OVERVIEW

OMORI.exe, made by thenyancatcookie, is meant to be a parody of the infamous .exe creepypasta genre. The mod introduces a distorted vesion of HeadSpace, the three party members AUBREY, KEL and HERO are separated from eachother as OMORI is nowhere to be seen, and now they must find a way to get out of the madness fallen over the world.

3. STORY

The mod opens with a cutscene of dialogues from Basil's POV, it goes as follows:

"I was the first to notice something off
I was never able to tell exactly what was wrong
but the feeling in my gut lingered anyway
it worsened the more time went by.
I kept a smile on my face, but my heart remained weary.
Why was I reacting this way?
Everything should be fine, right?
That's what he assured me.
"Everything is okay."
But the way he said it...
Made me think I was staring into the eyes of a stranger.

After the cutscene, the game tells us to "Select A File." with a screen similar to the Sonic.exe act selection. The contents of OMORI.exe is divided into four ACTS, with each ACT only being available after completing the previous one.
